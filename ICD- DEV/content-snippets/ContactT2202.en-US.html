As of January 1, 2019, the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) requires not-for-profit and educational institutions to include a Social Insurance Number (SIN) with all filings of the T2202 Education and Textbook Amounts Certificate form. The ICD must provide this information to the CRA in order to issue course participants a receipt for income tax purposes. If you require a T2202 tax receipt, please check the box above, and enter a valid SIN number below.
