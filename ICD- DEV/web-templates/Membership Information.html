About ICD Members

ICD is proud to be the membership organization for Canada’s community of directors. Our members serve on boards across every sector of Canada’s economy, be it for-profit, Crown or not-for-profit.

​ICD is represented in every province, through our local Chapter leadership and we encourage our members to get involved.

ICD members are accomplished business leaders who are committed to the highest standards of directorship. As a member of ICD, they benefit from and contribute to the best in class governance education, thought leadership and peer-to-peer networking.
